Evdanco is Romanian company with its main activity in the field of Oil and Gas. Specifically, EVDANCO provides for the design, modernization and repair, service, operation of land drilling and workover rigs. EVDANCO’s team is comprised of high experienced specialists who are committed to creating strong, long-life relationships with its partners and customers by focusing on the following: personal service; responsiveness; performance; reliability.
